SmartShop

This project was part of our school's entrepreneurship project challenge. We had to come up with a range of diverse problems and solutions to combat them.

Imagine you're trying to make lunch or dinner, but everytime you go to a grocery store, you buy too little or splurge and buy too much, spending far more money than you intended to. This app keeps track of the groceries in your pantry and the ingredients you need to make various recipes. When you want to make a recipe, the app tells you how much you need to buy, given what you have at home.

It's as easy as 1-2-3:

  1. Log your groceries in the app when you come home
  2. Select the recipe you want to make
  3. See how much of each ingredient you need to purchase
Avoid spending more time and money than you need to at the grocery store.
